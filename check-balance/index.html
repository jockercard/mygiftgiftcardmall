How to Check Gift Card Balance at GiftCardMall/MyGift

Checking your gift card balance at GiftCardMall/MyGift is quick and free. Our Gift Card Mall balance checker works around the clock so you can check my gift card balance whenever you need to — before shopping online, heading to a store, or splitting a payment between cards.

Steps to Check Your Gift Card Balance

  • Locate your card details: Find the 16-digit number on the front, the expiry date (MM/YY), and the 3-digit CVV on the back of your Visa gift card.
  • Enter the information above: Type your card number, expiration, and CVV into the check gift card balance form on this page.
  • View your results: Click "Check Gift Card Balance" to instantly see your available funds and recent purchase history.

Why Check Your Gift Card Balance Regularly?

Keeping track of your gift card balance helps you avoid declined payments at checkout. Some retailers, gas stations, and hotels place temporary authorization holds that can exceed your actual purchase amount. By checking your balance beforehand through GiftCardMall/MyGift, you can plan your spending and ensure smooth transactions every time.

Beyond checking your balance, the Gift Card Mall portal also allows you to register your gift card for online purchases, activate new cards, set up a personal PIN, and find answers to common questions. Everything you need to manage your prepaid Visa gift card is available in one place.
