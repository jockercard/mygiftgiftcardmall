Frequently Asked Questions

How do I check my gift card balance?

Enter your card number, expiration date, and security code (CVV) in the form on our homepage. Click "Check Balance" to view your available balance instantly.

Which cards can I check?

GC Check supports Visa, Mastercard, and American Express prepaid and gift cards issued through our platform.

Is my information secure?

Yes. We use industry-standard encryption to protect your card details. Your information is never stored and is transmitted securely.

What if my balance doesn't display?

Please verify your card number, expiration date, and CVV are entered correctly. If the issue persists, contact our support team.
